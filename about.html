A brief History of Lopez, Quezon

The history of the Municipality of Lopez reflects the path its leaders took to its present development. A prosperous and one of the first class municipality in the Fourth Congressional District of Quezon Province, Lopez has a total land area of 39,510 hectares, and now comprising of 88 rural barangays and seven (7) urban barangays with a total population of 95,167 in 2015.

The original location of the settlement was in the marshy mouth of a sedate river, which was founded by the descendants of Datu Dumangsil and Balingsula of the Kalilayan Kingdom.
