Process of Getting Document(s) If Printed by Your Own

  1. Download the online document portal by clicking on the download file button.
  2. Fill out the required fields with your personal information manually.
  3. Print your document(s) supplied to you via email.
  4. Compile the necessary documents that must be presented. They may contain legitimate identification cards, proof of residence, and other supplementary materials.
  5. Visit the barangay office to obtain payment, the barangay seal, and the signature of the barangay captain. Please bring the appropriate paperwork.
  6. Look for the secretary or designated individual responsible for handling your document application. Send the appropriate money and other documents to conclude the process.
  7. Wait for the release of your desired document following processing.

Process of Getting Document(s) If You Cannot Print on Your Own

  1. Access the online document portal by clicking on the provided link.
  2. Fill out the required fields with your personal information manually.
  3. Visit the Barangay to obtain the required document(s).
  4. Compile the necessary documents that must be presented. They may contain legitimate identification cards, proof of residence, and other supplementary materials.
  5. Visit the barangay office to obtain payment, the barangay seal, and the signature of the barangay captain. Be sure to carry the necessary documentation.
  6. Look for the secretary or designated individual responsible for handling your document application. Send the appropriate money and other documents to conclude the process.
  7. Wait for the release of your desired document following processing.
