Eastern Tayabas College Inc.

Eastern Tayabas College (ETC) is a non-sectarian, state college founded in 1953. Their mission is to provide quality education for the holistic development of the youth through adapting a curriculum using different strategies and using the latest framework to keep up with the development of civilization.

Takoyoshi

Authentic Japanese Takoyaki located at Brgy. Rizal Pob. Lopez, Quezon Open every day from 1 PM to 7 PM. Available for dine-in, take-out, and delivery.
