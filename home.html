Message from the Barangay Captain
Hon. Reynaldo A. Salumbides Jr.

I extend a warm welcome to all visitors to our Barangay's official website. As the elected Barangay Captain, it brings me great pleasure to provide you with a platform where you can gain insight into our community, its programs, services, and upcoming events. Our Barangay is characterized by its vibrancy and diversity, and our primary goal is to foster a safe, secure, and inclusive environment for all residents.

We are dedicated to delivering essential services such as healthcare, education, and public safety, and we continuously strive to enhance the quality of life within our Barangay. Through this website, you will discover valuable information regarding our Barangay's history, our elected officials, and the various initiatives we have undertaken to promote community development.

Additionally, you will find updates on forthcoming activities, events, and programs designed to benefit our residents. As your Barangay Captain, I am committed to serving our community and ensuring that the needs of our residents are met. Should you have any inquiries, concerns, or feedback, please do not hesitate to contact me or any of our Barangay officials.

Thank you for taking the time to visit our website, and I trust that you will find the information provided here both useful and informative.


Sincerely,

ABOUT OUR BARANGAY

Location of the barangay and its mission and vision

Mission

Pangangalagaan at itataguyod ang mga karapatan ng Lopezeño sa kalidad at matuwid na serbisyo kung saan ang ganap na programa ukol sa EDUKASYON, ALTERNATIBONG KABUHAYAN, PANGANGALAGA SA KALUSUGAN at MALUSOG NA KAPALIGIRAN ay pananagutan ng mapagkakatiwalaan at makatarungang mga pinuno upang mapanatili ang maunlad na pamayanan.

Vision

Isang Barangay na may takot sa Diyos, sumaksi sa malinaw at pantay na pamamahala upang matugunan ang mga mithiin tungo sa isang mapayapa, mabunga at maunlad na pamayanan.

The Barangay of Rizal (Poblacion) is located in Lopez, a municipality in the province of Quezon. Barangay RIZAL, Poblacion has a total area of approximately (117,998.25) square meters or 5.83 hectares. Even the smallest LGU the barangay creates its own Budget. It is not dependent on handouts from the city, municipality, or province.
